------------------------------------------------------------
[3] Mining / WebSocket endpoints
------------------------------------------------------------
*** NONE ***

Specifically:
- No `wss://` URLs anywhere in the APK.
- No `stratum+tcp://` or `stratum+ssl://` URLs.
- No `wss://api.cryptobrowser.site/api/v2/ws` (the desktop's mining WebSocket).
- No xmrig signatures, no Stratum protocol tokens.

Mining is delivered as a web page (`cryptobrowser.site/mobile/android`) loaded inside the browser's own webview/tab. The native binary plays no role.
