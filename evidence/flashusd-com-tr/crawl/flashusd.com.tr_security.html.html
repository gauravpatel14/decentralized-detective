Security Disclaimer

SECURITY ALERT: FLASH USDT will never ask for your private keys or seed phrases. Any website or person requesting such information is a scam.

1. Technical Infrastructure

Our gateways utilize AES-256 military-grade encryption for all socket communications and transaction data processing. This ensures that payment details remain confidential between the user and our on-chain settlement engine.

2. On-Chain Verification

Every FLASH USDT transaction is verified via public blockchain explorers. Our internal oracle v4.2 provides a secondary layer of real-time multi-chain status tracking to prevent double-spending and ensure liquidity accuracy.

3. Multi-Chain Protection

We operate dedicated nodes for TRC20, ERC20, and BEP20. This isolation prevents cross-chain vulnerability leakage. In the event of a network-specific exploit (e.g., a TRON network issue), our other chains remain operative.

4. User Self-Custody

Users are encouraged to use hardware wallets or reputable self-custody solutions (Metamask, Trust Wallet, TronLink). FLASH USDT does not provide custodial services and is not responsible for the security of your local device or third-party wallet provider.

5. FlashLoan Settlement

Our FlashLoan repayment mechanisms are handled by automated smart contracts. These contracts are audited for mathematical correctness to ensure that credit obligations and payouts are executed with deterministic precision.
